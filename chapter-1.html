angels dance upon skyscrapers of the heart.

I haven't written many love poems since I've been with you, I squirm alone, frown alone, revive myself alone, loathe myself and snatch myself away. Yet, not a single line of poetry emerges.

Suffering isn't poetry, but usually, I at least feel inspired, So angry that I write in my diary, So sad that I stand and make peace.

But I sit in silence, feeling happy but unsure, Joyful but then feeling guilty, In love but often feeling mismatched, Wanting you close but feeling repulsive.

Not sure what I want or what's right, Too deep to tell anyone that I've changed my mind, Everyone's advice is so good, but it never sticks, I always say I'll reach for the stars, but then I'm always content with star-shaped flowers.

I tell myself I'll change today, I said that yesterday, and the week before, month before, year before and years before.

I've said I want to change, countless times so many it feels impossible now.
